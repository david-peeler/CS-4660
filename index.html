Student-Facing AI Verification Lab

AI Arena: Investigate the Claim

Work through a set of classroom AI scenarios where you inspect suspicious claims, test generated code, match evidence to the right verifier, and decide what responsible AI use looks like in practice.

Overview

How this lab works

  • Each case gives you one scenario about CS hallucinations, prompt surgery, evidence matching, or code verification.
  • Submit the strongest decision you can defend with evidence, not just the fastest guess.
  • Hints are available when you need them, but they recede once your judgment tracker becomes steady.
  • After every case, log one quick reflection about the move that helped or the strategy you want to try next.
  • Use the classroom framework to separate helpful support from AI doing the work for you.
  • Your evidence score and judgment tracker update as you work through the review.
